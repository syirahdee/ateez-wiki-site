Song Mingi (Korean: 송민기; born August 9, 1999), mononymously known as Mingi, is a South Korean rapper and dancer. He is a member of the boy group Ateez, formed by KQ Entertainment.

EARLY LIFE

He attended Baekseok High School and graduated from Soora High School. He later joined KQ Entertainment as a trainee to become an idol.
