Kang Yeosang (Korean: 강여상; born June 15th, 1999), mononymously known as Yeosang, is a South Korean singer, dancer, and model. He is a member of the South Korean boy group ATEEZ under KQ Entertainment.

EARLY LIFE

Kang Yeosang was born June 15th, 1999, in Pohang, South Korea. Driven to enter the K-pop industry, Yeosang trained for years before making his official debut. A former BigHit Music trainee, he spent a considerable amount of time perfecting his skills, particularly vocal training and dance performance. Despite an initial setback when his ambition was opposed by his parents, he nurtured a passion for performing that led him to train before joining BigHit. A talent scout from KQ Entertainment approached him, initiating an interview and audition process. Yeosang made a conscious decision to balance school and training, committing fully to KQ, and eventually debuting with ATEEZ. He spoke candidly about walking away from BigHit in a TV program, showing that despite those moments of rejection, his dedication and pursuit allowed him to move forward. Yeosang is an idol who shows true passion for performance.

2018: KQ FELLAZ, DEBUT IN ATEEZ

On October 24, 2018, Yeosang debuted as a member of KQ Entertainment's first boy group, ATEEZ, with the release of the mini album Treasure EP.1 : All to Zero.

2021-2024: THE SHOW

On February 19, 2021, it was confirmed that Yeosang, WEi's Kim Yo-han, and Weeekly's Jihan would be the new MCs for the SBS MTV show The Show. It marked his first application to be MC on the SBS MTV broadcast channel.

On March 14, 2023, it was announced that WEi's Kim Yo-han and Weeekly's Jihan would be leaving the show. Yeosang would continue with new MCs Xiaojun and Hyeongjun.

On March 26 and April 2, 2024, it was confirmed that Yeosang will be stepping down as MC, along with his co-hosts Xiaojun and CRAVITY's Hyeongjun.

On April 2, 2024, Hyeongjun replacing CRAVITY's Hyeongjun, NCT's Xiaojun and Yeosang end hosting show as SBS MTV The Show MCs.

2024-2025: MAGAZINE APPEARANCE

In April 2024, Yeosang achieved a major fashion milestone by making his first-ever cover appearance for Men's Folio, a prestigious fashion magazine. This exclusive cover showcase featured a striking conceptual photoshoot with a stylish, modern aesthetic showcasing a classic urban theme set in Singapore, including iconic architecture such as Marina Bay Sands and Singapore Flyer. This cover story and photoshoot allowed fans to discover more about his artistic journey and personal growth behind his role as an ATEEZ member, showcasing both his dynamic performer identity and subtle charisma through high fashion editorial looks.

Highlighting his visual versatility for the issue, Men's Folio Singapore shared a key theme, expressing sentiment during the shooting, noting that "it's for the overall concept that we want to bring out in this issue, to serve as a reminder that we can always return to..." for conveying the essence of these precious memories and how to cherish female fans and viewers.

His major solo endeavor for the year, the portfolio feature, garnered high-impact media attention through his first-ever solo pictorial feature with Cosmopolitan Korea. Moving away from standard pose setups, the shoot gave the star a platform to showcase a unique side that felt both captivating and artistic. It created a powerful visual concept that highlighted raw and authentic textures directly from the studio environment; the team praised the photoshoot, describing Yeosang's styling as effortlessly classic and praising his demeanor during production breaks, highlighting his relaxed energy and natural charm on set throughout the shoot.

The accompaniment editorial interview allowed Yeosang to reflection deeply on his personal growth, professional mindset, and career experiences, expressing profound insights on his growth process and clear vision for his future as he continues to push boundaries and further build his position both as an artist and as part of ATEEZ's global footprint. Furthermore, when asked to reflect on his artistic journey, he offered a thoughtful message to his fans, expressing gratitude for their support and acknowledging the fans' role in shaping his growth as an artist, noting that he intends to ultimate express gratitude to the fans and team who have stood by him and supported his journey through high-level art.

SONGWRITING CREDITS

On December 8 at midnight, Yeosang surprised and delighted fans worldwide by officially releasing the music video for his solo track "Legacy" on ATEEZ's official YouTube channel. The dramatic visual concept instantly set a cinematic tone, combining striking urban aesthetics, dramatic lighting, and a blend of raw, intense vocal expressions to bring the memorable melody of "Legacy" to life. Strong choreography highlights step through the dynamic performance, featuring fluid yet sharp movements that emphasize his technical precision and stage presence. Accompanied by dark and powerful instrumental production, the MV created a visually stunning, polished aesthetic that captivated fans around the world from start to finish.

"Legacy" served as Yeosang's highly anticipated solo project within the group's GOLDEN HOUR : Part.2 album release, which marked a significant milestone in his artistic journey. Beyond demonstrating his evolving performance capabilities, Yeosang contributed directly to the creative direction, writing lyrics and creating a distinct individual identity for the musical narrative. Written in collaboration with fellow ATEEZ member and leader Hongjoong, who helped co-produce the track, "Legacy" reflects Yeosang's personal style, combining sleek production, energetic rap delivery, and captivating vocal melodies that showcase his growth as a versatile artist. The project received widespread praise from fans and music critics alike for its high production quality, dramatic storytelling, and the seamless integration of dance with dynamic musical hooks.

Even before the official release of the music video, Yeosang had already established "Legacy" as a major highlight during ATEEZ's 2024 world tour, "TOWARDS THE LIGHT : WILL TO POWER." Performing the song live on tour across North America, Japan, and Europe, Yeosang captivated audiences with his commanding stage presence, crisp vocal delivery, and powerful solo performance. The live renditions earned massive praise for their high-energy delivery, magnetic stage presence, and visual flair, solidifying the track's status as a fan favorite long before the studio version was officially released.

"IT's You" marks a significant milestone in Yeosang's career as the first official unit track featuring his creative contribution as a songwriter and performer. Released on December 1, 2023, as a standout track on their record-breaking studio album, THE WORLD EP.FIN : WILL, the track features a sleek, futuristic sound that distinguished itself from the album's high-energy production. Pairing Yeosang with fellow members Yunho and Wooyoung, "IT's You" delivered a hypnotic performance that showcased the trio's unique vocal chemistry, intricate unit choreography, smooth vocal harmonies, and artistic synergy within ATEEZ's expanding musical discography.

The creation of the track was an deeply collaborative effort, with Yeosang, Yunho, and Wooyoung actively contributing directly to the song's lyrics and vocal arrangements. Working alongside their in-house production team, the unit created a distinct narrative centered around themes of longing and devotion; Yeosang's smooth vocal tones served as a crucial anchor for the track's atmospheric production. This hands-on involvement ensured that the track's romantic, late-night vibe resonated genuinely with the trio's musical identities, giving fans a deeper connection to their growth as artists and songwriters.

To further elevate the aura of the album, a dedicated music video was released on January 17, 2024, showcasing an official cinematic visual conceptualized for the track. The video features an sleek futuristic urban aesthetic, utilizing atmospheric lighting, artistic set designs, and smooth camera transitions to complement the song's intimate narrative tone. The performance aspect of the MV showcased fluid yet precise choreography, capturing the distinct charisma of all three members and solidifying the song's status as a standout favorite among fans.
