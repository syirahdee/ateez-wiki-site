Choi San (Korean: 최산; born July 10, 1999), mononymously known as San, is a South Korean singer, dancer, model and ambassador. He is a member of the South Korean boy group ATEEZ under KQ Entertainment.

San debuted as a vocalist of ATEEZ on October 24, 2018, with the release of the mini album Treasure EP.1: All to Zero. Known for his powerful vocals, fluid choreography, and distinct stage presence, San has since become a major contributor to ATEEZ's success.

Beyond group activities, he has established himself through solo projects, fashion partnerships, and individual broadcast appearances.

EARLY LIFE

Choi San was born on July 10, 1999, in Jinju, Gyeongsangnam-do, South Korea. Growing up, San developed an interest in performance, which eventually led him to pursue a career in the entertainment industry.

San attended Korean Arts High School, graduating in 2018. During his school years, he focused on building his vocal and dance foundation prior to joining KQ Entertainment, where he trained as part of the agency's upcoming boy group.

2018: PRE-DEBUT

Prior to his debut, San trained under KQ Entertainment as part of the trainee team KQ Fellaz. As part of the pre-debut promotions, he appeared in the reality series KQ Fellaz American Training, which documented the group's journey to Los Angeles, California, to receive professional dance instruction and performance coaching. The series culminated in the release of the pre-debut performance video "From," introducing San and his fellow trainees to the public before their official debut.

2018–PRESENT: DEBUT AND ACTIVITIES WITH ATEEZ

San officially debuted as a member of ATEEZ on October 24, 2018, with the dual lead singles "Pirate King" and "Treasure" from their debut mini album Treasure EP.1: All to Zero.

As a main vocalist and prominent performer for ATEEZ, San has contributed to the group's extensive discography, participating in domestic releases as well as international Japanese and global albums. Over the years, ATEEZ has gained widespread commercial and critical acclaim, achieving top positions on charts such as the Billboard 200 and performing at major venues and music festivals worldwide.

San's dynamic vocals and expressive stage presence have consistently been highlighted in ATEEZ's title tracks, music videos, and live performances, contributing significantly to the group's international fanbase and performance reputation.

2021–PRESENT: ACTING DEBUT AND SOLO PROJECTS

In May 2021, San made his acting debut in the KBS2 television drama series Imitation. The series, based on the webtoon of the same name, depicted the lives of idols in the South Korean entertainment industry. San portrayed Min-soo, a member of the fictional idol group SHAX, appearing alongside fellow ATEEZ members Seonghwa, Yunho, and Jongho.

In addition to acting, San has expanded his solo profile through individual television and digital appearances. He has featured as a guest on several variety programs, including Running Man, Workman, and various online content series, where his personality and adaptability earned positive audience reception.

In June 2024, San was named official ambassador for Jinju City, his hometown, in recognition of his contributions to promoting the region through his cultural reach as an international artist. During the appointment ceremony held at Jinju City Hall, he was awarded an official commission plaque and expressed his intention to support local heritage initiatives and community causes. His role includes involvement in promoting the city's signature cultural events, such as the Jinju Namgang Yudeung Festival, as well as participating in public interest campaigns aimed at youth and regional development.

San has also been active in philanthropic work, contributing to charitable causes both individually and alongside ATEEZ.

SONGWRITING CREDITS

In 2023, San co-wrote lyrics for "IT's You", a unit track featured on ATEEZ's studio album THE WORLD EP.FIN: WILL. The track, performed by San, Yeosang, and Wooyoung, marked his official entry as a credited lyricist under the Korea Music Copyright Association (KOMCA).

"IT's You" was accompanied by an official music video released in early 2024, which received praise for its sleek concept and unit performance dynamics.

In 2024, San expanded his songwriting credentials with his solo track "Empty Box," featured on ATEEZ's mini album GOLDEN HOUR: Part.1. San participated in writing the lyrics, delivering a reflective R&B-infused vocal track that highlighted his vocal range and emotive interpretation.

San continues to explore musical composition and lyricism, expressing interest in expanding his creative contributions to future group and solo projects.

ENDORSEMENTS

San's distinct fashion style and strong physical presence have led to high-profile involvement in the global fashion industry, where he has represented major luxury brands.

Fashion

San's entry into high fashion accelerated in 2024 when he was invited to attend the Dolce & Gabbana Alta Sartoria show in Sardinia, Italy. His appearance in a custom architectural piece from the brand generated extensive social media coverage and fashion media attention.

San subsequently attended subsequent events hosted by the Italian luxury house, including high fashion showcases in Milan and Paris.

In September 2024, Dolce & Gabbana officially appointed San as a Global Brand Ambassador, making him one of the few K-pop artists to represent the house worldwide. The announcement highlighted San's modern elegance, powerful stage presence, and natural alignment with the brand's aesthetic. As global ambassador, San has featured in international campaigns, brand events, and editorial features across major fashion publications.

In November 2024, he attended the Dolce & Gabbana "Beauty" launch event in Seoul, joining international figures in promoting the brand's luxury fragrance and cosmetics line.

His ongoing partnership with Dolce & Gabbana is recognized as one of the key milestones in establishing his personal brand in global fashion.

PHILANTHROPY

San has consistently engaged in charitable activities, quietly supporting community programs and relief initiatives both individually and together with ATEEZ.

In March 2022, San made a personal donation to support emergency relief efforts for communities affected by severe wildfires in the Gangwon and Gyeongsangbuk-do regions. His contribution helped provide temporary housing, food, and essential supplies to displaced families.

In April 2023, San participated in a charity auction held by Hope Bridge Korea Disaster Relief Association, donating personal performance wardrobe items to raise funds for disaster-vulnerable populations.

In December 2023, he contributed to local welfare initiatives in his hometown of Jinju, supporting winter heating assistance funds and educational support programs for low-income youth.

PERSONAL LIFE

San is known for his close relationship with his family and often expresses appreciation for his hometown, Jinju. He practices taekwondo and holds a black belt, a background he credits for his physical discipline and balance on stage.
