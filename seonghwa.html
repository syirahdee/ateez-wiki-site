Park Seonghwa (Korean: 박성화 ; born April 3rd, 1998), mononymously known as Seonghwa, is a South Korean vocalist, rapper, performer, visual and actor. He is the oldest and a member of South Korean boy band ATEEZ, debuting on October 24, 2018.

EARLY LIFE

Park Seonghwa was born April 3rd, 1998, in Jinju, Gyeongnam, South Korea. He attended Myungshin Highschool before enrolling at the Global Cyber University. Later, he joined KQ Entertainment as a trainee to become an idol. In October 2017, a few of KQ Entertainment's trainees took part in a casting for the show MIXNINE (a survival reality show). Unfortunately, Seonghwa didn't make it.

CAREER

2018: KQ FELLAZ, ATEEZ

Before debuting, Seonghwa was introduced as part of "KQ Fellaz", which is a pre-debut team of trainees under KQ. On October 24, 2018, Seonghwa debuted as a member of ATEEZ with the title tracks "Treasure" and "Pirate King". In the group, he holds the vocalist and visual positions.

2020-2021: ACTING DEBUT

Among the notable additions to the lineup was Ateez's Seonghwa, who stepped into the acting world to play the role of Seyoung—the dedicated and charismatic leader of the fictional K-pop boy group Sparkling.

Seonghwa made fashion history as the first Asian celebrity appointed as the global ambassador and face of Isabel Marant's menswear line, Marant Homme, specifically fronting their Spring-Summer 2025 campaign.

When Seonghwa made his runway debut for Isabel Marant's Fall/Winter 2025 collection at Paris Fashion Week this March, he didn't just turn heads; he captivated the entire room.

Seonghwa effortlessly embodies this fusion of edge, elegance, and skilled androgyny. His latest ambassadorship with French label Isabel Marant showcases his ability to merge bold styling with refined elegance.

Following his appointment in July 2025, Seonghwa made history as the first-ever global ambassador for the luxury South Korean avant-garde designer brand SONGZIO. This active artistic partnership goes beyond standard endorsements, with Seonghwa fronting major campaigns like the Fall/Winter collections and starring as the cover model for DAZED Korea's digital issues in collaboration with the fashion house.

A frequent fixture at Paris Fashion Week, Seonghwa has walked for the brand during three consecutive seasons to showcase its signature dark, avant-garde designs. He made his debut on June 19, 2025, for the Spring/Summer 2026 "Polyptych" show, followed by the Autumn/Winter 2026 presentation titled "Crushed, Cast, Constructed." Most recently, on June 24, 2026, he returned to the runway for the Spring/Summer 2027 "IMPAVIDE" collection, where he modeled two distinct looks.
